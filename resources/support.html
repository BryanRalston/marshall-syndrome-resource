Genetic Counseling

If not already done, the family should pursue formal genetic counseling to:

  • Understand the inheritance pattern (autosomal dominant — 50% chance of passing to each child)
  • Interpret their specific mutation and its known associations
  • Discuss family planning implications
  • Connect to appropriate registries and research
